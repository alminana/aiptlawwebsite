Pakistan

Trademark Registration

Requirements:

  • The applicant's particulars: name, address and nationality
  • Notarized and Legalized power of attorney up to the Pakistani Consulate in the domicile of the applicant.
  • The copy of the trademark
  • Classes of the goods and services
Time for a trademark registration: 30 Months

Patent Registration

Requirements:

  • The applicant's particulars: name, address and nationality.
  • The inventor's particulars: name, address and nationality.
  • Simply signed POA required.
  • Simply signed Deed of assignment required.
  • Certified Priority documents ( if claimed).

Industrial Design Registration

Requirements:

  • The applicant's particulars: name, address and nationality
  • The designer's particulars: name, address and nationality
  • Simply signed power of attorney
  • Formal drawings showing every possible view of the design
  • Priority document, if any

IP Treaties Membership:

Paris Convention, Berne Convention

Nepal

Trademark Registration

Requirements:

  • The applicant's particulars: name, address and nationality.
  • Notarized Power of Attorney, it can be submitted within 35 days from the date of filing.
  • Certified copy of the Priority Documents, it can be submitted within 35 days from the date of filing if claimed.
  • Classes and specification of goods/services.
  • A copy of the trademark.
Time for trademark registration: 12 months

Patent Registration

Requirements:

Malaysia

Trademark Registration

Requirements:

  • The applicant's particulars: name, address and nationality
  • Notarized Power of Attorney, (Malaysia TMO do not require POA to be submitted for trademark application, but the Trade Marks Registry will however require a Statutory Declaration to be submitted)
  • A certified and legalized copy of priority application (if claimed)
  • The list of the goods/services
  • Classes and specification of goods/services
  • A copy of the trademark
Time for trademark registration: 14 months

Note:

  • Multiple Class Registration is available
  • No Extra Fees for Color/Design Mark
  • Extra fees for long Goods/Services
